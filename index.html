Client BeatMap

BeatMap is like airbnb for events. People can rent out venues like their terrace, garage space or any other space for conducting events.

The app will have a list of events happening nearby as well as venues available for renting. The app is primarily targeted at small size performance events such as a band’s music performance, standup comedy, storytelling, speeches etc. A user can open the app to find events and spaces available for rent near him.
